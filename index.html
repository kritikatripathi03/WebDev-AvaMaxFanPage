About

Have you ever heard the song "Sweet but Psycho"? Or "Kings & Queens"? Or possibly, her latest single, "Choose Your Fighter"? Well, you probably didn't know that the voice behind these songs was none other than the phenomenal Ava Max. Max was born on February 16, 1994 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. In 2008, she began posting material on MySpace at the age of 14. She then released an EP titled Amanda Kay later that year which featured six original songs that gained barely any traction. In 2013, she released an independent single titled "Take Away the Pain" which was later remixed and re-released by Canadian DJ/producer duo Project 46 in 2015. In the same year, Max started releasing songs on SoundCloud under the name AVA, one of them being "Anyone But You" which helped kick off her career and caught the eye of many major record labels. It wasn't until August 2018 that she released "Sweet but Psycho", her official debut single, through Atlantic Records. Upon release, it became a sleeper hit, appearing on various Spotify playlists and subsequently reaching number one in 22 countries including Germany and the United Kingdom. Her debut studio album, Heaven & Hell, was released in September 2020, and spawned several singles including her second Billboard Hot 100 entry, "Kings & Queens".
